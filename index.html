About Us

Welcome to Viea, a charming bakery nestled in the heart of Eldoria, where the sweet scent of delectable delights beckons you to indulge in an unforgettable culinary experience. Established by the passionate and talented owner, Isabella, Viea is a celebration of time-honored recipes infused with creative innovation.

Step into our warm and inviting space, adorned with vintage photographs that tell the story of Eldoria's history and the evolution of Viea. Our bakery is more than a place to savor mouthwatering pastries; it's a community hub where stories are shared, milestones celebrated, and memories created.
